Biscuits

Description

These light, fluffy 3 ingredient homemade biscuits are made easy by using Bisquick™ mix and can be ready to eat in 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups Bisquick™ Original Pancake & Baking Mix
  • 2/3 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 450°F. In medium bowl, stir together ingredients until soft dough forms.
  2. Drop dough by 8 spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheet.
  3. Bake 11 to 13 minutes or until golden brown. Serve warm.
